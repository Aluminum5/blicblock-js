How to Play

Create tetrominos to score points!

Blocks in six different colors —pink, orange, yellow green, blue, and white—will fall from the top.

Use ← and →, or a and d, to position a block as it falls.

Swipe left or right on the game board to position a block as it falls.

Use ↓ or s to drop a block immediately.

Swipe down on the game board to drop a block immediately.

Hit the spacebar to pause and resume the game.

Tap outside the game board to pause and resume the game.

The game ends when there is no more room for blocks to fall.
