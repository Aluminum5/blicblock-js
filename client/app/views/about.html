About BlicblockJS

Blicblock is a game your Sims can play in The Sims 4. After seeing my Sims play it enough on their computers, I realized it looked like a fully developed game idea, and not just bits of color flashing around on their screens.

Who made BlicblockJS?

I'm Sarah Vessels, a programmer in Lexington, Kentucky. I love The Sims series and programming, so I combined the two and made BlicblockJS!

Can I see the source code?

Sure! It's available on Github under the MIT license. Feedback and bug reports are welcome! Please report bugs here.
